Key Vocabulary (Lesson 1.1)

Use these words to explain your thinking.

Integer

A whole number that can be positive, negative, or zero (e.g., ‚Äì4, 0, 7).

Positive

A number greater than zero. Often represents a gain or increase.

Negative

A number less than zero. Often represents a loss or decrease.

Zero

The reference point: no gain and no loss.

Number Line

A visual model showing numbers increasing to the right and decreasing to the left.

Gain / Increase

A change that moves a value up or to the right on a number line.

Loss / Decrease

A change that moves a value down or to the left on a number line.

Reasoning

Explaining why an answer makes sense using words, diagrams, or math.
