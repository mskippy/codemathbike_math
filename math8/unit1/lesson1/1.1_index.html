Welcome to Math 8

Today is about getting started and understanding how math will work in this class. This is not a test. The focus is on thinking, explaining, and working together.

Course Overview

Math 8 focuses on building strong number sense, algebraic thinking, and problem-solving skills. You will work with integers, algebra, geometry, data, and real-world contexts.

Explaining your thinking and learning from mistakes are key parts of this course.

Class Expectations

  • Come prepared and ready to learn
  • Participate actively in group work
  • Show your thinking, not just answers
  • Ask questions and take learning risks
  • Respect others and the learning space

What We’re Doing in Class Today

KenKen Puzzles

KenKen puzzles help develop logical thinking, pattern recognition, and perseverance.

  • Each number appears once in every row and column
  • Numbers in a cage combine to match the target value
  • Operations may include +, −, ×, or ÷
  • There is more than one place to start — keep trying

Getting Ready for Math 8 Stations

You will work in groups through stations that review important Math 7 ideas. The goal is to show how you think, explain your reasoning, and work collaboratively.
